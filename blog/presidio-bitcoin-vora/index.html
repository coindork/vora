Self-Custody AI and Bitcoin

March 2026 · Vora

In October 2025, Erik Cason and Jesse Posner sat down with Presidio Bitcoin for an hour on the question at the center of the NYKNYM thesis: if we spent seventeen years learning to hold our own money, what happens when the thing we need to hold is not money but intelligence?

"Self-Custody AI and Bitcoin with Vora" — Presidio Bitcoin, October 20, 2025.

The conversation covers the full sovereignty stack — from Posner's hardware-layer argument (FPGA over opaque secure elements, open-source firmware, reproducible builds) to Cason's philosophical frame (cognition is upstream of everything, and if the AI that helps you think is custodial, the most important cognitive process in your life is controlled by an entity whose incentives are not yours). The most complete single-sitting articulation of the argument.
